Quality Over Quantity: A Developer's Perspective 🛠️ A small rant by @nyx69 (original creator of this bot). It is actually better NOT to have a rigid, public release date. It allows Realme engineers to release software only when it actually meets stability requirements. When tech companies are forced to meet strict public deadlines, they often release unfinished, bug-ridden software just to avoid investor backlash. We've seen this happen constantly in the gaming industry. The software engineers are working incredibly hard to maintain dozens of device models simultaneously. Fixing bugs, optimizing batteries, and porting Android versions takes massive amounts of code testing. They don't do this in five minutes. Companies have limited developer capacity, so minor fixes are often bundled together into larger, more stable monthly patches.
