Tips for a Healthy Battery 🔋 Modern smartphone batteries are highly optimized, but these habits can extend their overall lifespan: • Keep your charge between 20% and 80% for daily use when possible. • Avoid extreme heat: Do not charge your phone under direct sunlight or while playing heavy games on maximum brightness. • Use the "Optimized Charging" feature in your battery settings (this learns your sleep schedule and prevents the battery from sitting at 100% all night). • Restart your device once a week to clear system cache and prevent rogue background apps from draining power.
