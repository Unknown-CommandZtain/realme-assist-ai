Estimating Stable Release Dates 📆 Realme pushes a stable update when it passes strict quality assurance—not just to meet a deadline. Therefore, exact calendar dates for Stable rollouts do not exist. How to Estimate: Use the /android16 command to see when your device's Early Access (Beta) began. Add approximately 3 to 5 months to that date. This is the standard timeframe the software team needs to gather bug logs, patch system crashes, and polish the firmware from a Beta build into a Global Stable release. Please stand by!
