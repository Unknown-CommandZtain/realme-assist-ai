Official Support Ticket Submission 📝 If you have encountered a bug or critical system flaw that the community has been unable to resolve after troubleshooting, you can request direct developer intervention. The group administrators review these collected logs and structural summaries to escalate persistent issues directly to internal engineering teams. Submission Rules: • Do not spam or duplicate forms for the same issue. • Provide explicit, step-by-step reproduction metrics. • Attaching screenshots or screen recording logs via the *#800# feedback menu greatly speeds up system patches.
