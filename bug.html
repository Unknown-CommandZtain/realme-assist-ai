Official Bug Reporting ⚠️ If you face an issue that is clearly a system bug and cannot be resolved by the community, you need to let the official developers know so they can patch it. Please provide as much useful information and screen recording evidence as possible. Open your default Phone dialer and type: *#800# This will open the hidden System Feedback Toolkit. Select the category of your bug, start a screen recording log, reproduce the issue, and submit the log directly to the engineering team. Alternatively, you can use the Feedback section inside the official Realme Community App.
