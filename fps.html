High FPS & Mobile Game Optimization 🎮 Modern games are exceptionally resource-intensive, requiring your device's processor (CPU) and graphics chip (GPU) to render complex instructions in fractions of a millisecond. Hardware vs. Thermals Realme scales hardware parameters strictly based on internal thermal thresholds. While your processor might be physically capable of running a game at 90 or 120 FPS, the system firmware will dynamically cap frame rates if the battery or internal components approach unsafe temperatures to prevent permanent hardware degradation. Game Optimization Game developers must manually optimize their engines for individual mobile chips. If a brand-new flagship processor is underperforming on your favorite title, it typically means the game studio has not yet whitelisted the chipset for higher graphic configurations. Pro-Tip: Ensure you have "GT Mode" or "HyperBoost" toggled on within the Games app to allocate maximum system resources to active gaming sessions.
