App-Services Not Found Error ⚠️ Why am I seeing this error? This error typically surfaces when a user force-installs an absolute mismatched or incompatible system app APK (most commonly a Chinese region Phone Manager or System Launcher build) onto a Global ROM device. How to resolve it: • Open your device Settings -> Apps -> App Management. • Tap the three dots in the top-right corner and select "Show System". • Search for the app causing issues (e.g., Phone Manager), tap it, click the three dots, and select "Uninstall Updates". If the default system app manager is completely crashed and inaccessible, download a third-party application management tool like fooView or an APK installer app to manually target and remove the faulty update package.
