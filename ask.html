How to Ask for Help ❓ 1. Formulate the Question To get the best help, make sure your message includes: • The specific device you use (e.g., Realme 14 Pro+) • Your current Realme UI and Android Version • The version of the app causing the issue • Exactly what you are trying to do • What troubleshooting steps you have already tried • Any specific error messages you received 2. Wait for a Response Give the community up to 48 hours to answer your question. The exact expert who knows how to fix your issue might not be online immediately. 3. No Answer Yet? If you do not receive an answer after a few days, use the /form command and fill out the official support document. Following these guidelines allows us to provide better, faster answers and keeps the chat clean!
