Virtual RAM (DRE) 💾 Dynamic RAM Expansion (DRE) lets your phone use internal storage as virtual memory. While modern Realme devices feature incredibly fast UFS 3.1 and UFS 4.0 storage speeds, internal storage is still significantly slower than physical LPDDR5X RAM chips. Virtual RAM helps keep lightweight background apps alive, but it will not magically increase your gaming framerates or processing power. It acts as a safety net (a swap-file) for multitasking. It's a great feature, but don't expect it to turn a budget phone into a flagship! 😉
