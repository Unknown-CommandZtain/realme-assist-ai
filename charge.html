Modern Charging Speeds ⚡ Realme devices use SUPERVOOC technology, utilizing dual-cell batteries and advanced heat management chips to achieve massive charging speeds (up to 240W). Because SUPERVOOC alters voltage and amperage dynamically depending on heat and current battery percentage, a simple math formula won't give you exact times. However, here are real-world expectations: Standard SUPERVOOC (67W) • Fills a 5000mAh battery to 50% in ~18 minutes. • A full 100% charge takes roughly 45 minutes. Flagship SUPERVOOC (100W - 150W) • Fills a 5000mAh battery to 50% in ~10 minutes. • A full 100% charge takes about 25 minutes. Ultra-Fast SUPERVOOC (240W) • Can fully charge a 4600mAh dual-cell battery in under 10 minutes! Note: To achieve these maximum speeds, you must use the original charging brick and the proprietary high-current cable that came in the box.
