Benchmark Your Device 💪 For reference, we use two standard benchmarks to compare devices in this group. Please install them from the Play Store: • CPU Performance: Geekbench 6 ⬇ • GPU/Gaming: 3DMark (Wild Life) ⬇ Preparation: • Disable any power-saving modes. • Enable "GT Mode" or "Performance Mode" in battery settings. • Clear all open apps via your recent apps menu. • Turn on Airplane Mode to prevent background syncing. Run Geekbench 6 first and take a screenshot of the score. Repeat this process three times in a row to test sustained thermal performance. Close all applications, let your device cool down for 5 minutes, and then repeat the process for 3DMark. Upload your first and last scores as an album in @realme_offtopic and put the Android version and device model (e.g., #Android16 #RMX3850) in the caption!
