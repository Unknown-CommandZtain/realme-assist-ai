OTA Update Rollouts 📡 Don't worry if you haven't received an update yet 🤖 To ensure absolute system stability, all firmware updates utilize staged rollouts. • The update is randomly pushed to a very small percentage of users first. • The engineering team monitors these devices for critical bugs or battery drains. • If no issues appear within a few days, the full global rollout begins. Just leave your auto-update settings on and your device will notify you when your batch is ready!
